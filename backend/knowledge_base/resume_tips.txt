# Resume Writing Best Practices

## General Tips
- Keep your resume concise and focused
- Use action verbs to describe achievements
- Quantify results whenever possible
- Tailor your resume for each job application
- Proofread carefully for errors

## Format and Structure
- Use consistent formatting throughout
- Include clear section headings
- Use bullet points for readability
- Keep to 1-2 pages maximum
- Use professional fonts (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman)

## Content Sections
### Contact Information
- Full name
- Professional email
- Phone number
- Location (city, state)
- LinkedIn profile

### Professional Summary
- 2-3 sentences highlighting key qualifications
- Focus on relevant skills and experience
- Include industry-specific keywords
- Align with job requirements

### Work Experience
- Company name and location
- Job title and dates
- Achievement-focused bullet points
- Use metrics to demonstrate impact
- Focus on relevant responsibilities

### Education
- Degree and major
- University name and location
- Graduation date
- Relevant coursework
- Academic achievements

### Skills
- Technical skills
- Soft skills
- Industry-specific certifications
- Language proficiencies
- Tools and technologies

## Keywords and ATS
- Include relevant keywords from job description
- Use standard section headings
- Avoid tables and complex formatting
- Save in standard formats (PDF, DOCX)
- Test with ATS systems
